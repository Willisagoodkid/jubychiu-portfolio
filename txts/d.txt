Industrial Arc Floor Lamp

Illuminate your space with this striking arc floor lamp. Designed to provide overhead lighting without the need for ceiling suspension, it features a sweeping metal arm that extends gracefully over sofas or dining tables.

The base is a solid block of Carrara marble, ensuring stability and adding a touch of luxury. The spun aluminum shade includes a diffuser to cast a soft, ambient glow, perfect for reading or creating a cozy atmosphere.

Materials: Stainless Steel, Carrara Marble, Aluminum
Dimensions: 220cm H x 200cm W (Adjustable)
