Nordic Wishbone Dining Chair

Inspired by classic Danish design, this dining chair combines traditional craftsmanship with modern functionality. The iconic Y-shaped backrest offers excellent support while maintaining a light and airy profile.

The seat is hand-woven from durable paper cord, providing a comfortable and flexible sitting experience. Finished in natural oak, this chair brings a touch of organic warmth and simplicity to any dining area or study.

Materials: Natural Oak, Paper Cord
Dimensions: 55cm W x 52cm D x 75cm H
